How To Enroll

In simple terms, here is how the Medicaid-funded transportation system works in New York:

  1. You must ask your care provider to schedule transportation services through Medicaid, or they determine you need them and inform you of this determination.
  2. At least three days before your appointment, your care provider must contact Medical Answering Services to make a request for non-emergncy medical transportation.
  3. Your care provider fills out Form 2015 from Medical Answering Services
  4. You call Medical Answering Services and request a specific transportation provider. At this point in the process you can request GCC Transportation as your trusted transportation provider.
  5. Your trusted transportation provider does your scheduled trips to and from your medical appointment.
